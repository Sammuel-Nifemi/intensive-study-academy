    Admission Requirements

    • Master’s degree in a relevant field from a recognised institution.
    • Minimum average score of 60% or CGPA of at least 3.5 on a 5-point scale.
    • Well-articulated research proposal.
    • Academic transcripts for all previous qualifications.
    • Evidence of research capability (publications where available).
    • Valid means of identification.
    • Recent passport photograph.
    • Completed online application form.
    • Active email address and phone/WhatsApp number.

    Note: PhD programmes are research-intensive. Coursework requirements, where applicable, are determined by the department and supervisor.
