    Admission Requirements

    • Bachelor’s degree with a minimum of Second Class Lower.
    • Relevant degree from a recognised institution.
    • Postgraduate Diploma (PGD) may be accepted where applicable.
    • Academic transcripts.
    • NYSC discharge or exemption certificate (where applicable).
    • Valid means of identification.
    • Recent passport photograph.
    • Completed online application form.
    • Active email address and phone/WhatsApp number.
